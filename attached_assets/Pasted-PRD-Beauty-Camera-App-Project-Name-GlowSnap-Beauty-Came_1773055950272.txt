PRD – Beauty Camera App
Project Name

GlowSnap – Beauty Camera & Selfie Filters

1. Product Overview

GlowSnap is a mobile beauty camera application that allows users to capture photos and videos with built-in beauty filters, makeup effects, and photo editing tools.

The app focuses on providing simple, fast, and high-quality selfie enhancements without using artificial intelligence. Users can apply filters, beauty effects, stickers, and editing tools while taking photos or editing pictures from their gallery.

The goal is to create a lightweight and easy-to-use beauty camera that helps users take attractive selfies and share them on social media.
